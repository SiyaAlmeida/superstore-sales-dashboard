# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard

## 🔍 Overview
This project presents a sales dashboard built using Excel/Google Sheets to analyze retail data from a Superstore dataset. It provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and the impact of discounts.

---
## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Superstore Orders Dataset (Kaggle)

The dataset includes detailed retail transaction data such as:
- Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date  
- Customer Name, Segment, Region  
- Product Category & Sub-category  
- Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount  
- Shipping details and more  

This dataset is used to analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior.
---

## ⚙️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets  
- Pivot Tables  
- Data Visualization Techniques  
- Dashboard Design  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Total Orders
  - Average Discount  
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis  
- Sales Distribution by Category  
- Top 10 Customers by Sales  
- Profit Margin by Sub-category  
- Impact of Discount on Profit  
- Sales vs Profit Comparison  
- Interactive Filters (Slicers)

## 💡 Key Insights
- Technology and Furniture categories contribute the highest share of total sales  
- High discounts negatively impact profit and may lead to losses  
- A small group of top customers generates a large portion of revenue  
- Some sub-categories have negative profit margins despite good sales  
- Sales show variation across months, indicating seasonal trends
